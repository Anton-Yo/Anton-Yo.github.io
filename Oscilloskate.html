Oscilloskate

Oscilloskate invites the player to track and follow a wavelength, displayed on the patented L Stands for Winner Oscilloscope. The oscilloscope's wavelength swaps between analog, static and digital mode adjusting the music accordingly, keeping the player on their toes. This game was developed over 48 hours for the Macquarie Computing Society Game Jam (2024) but unfortunately had a last minute bug and missed submission. Play on itch.io.

My Contributions:

  • Movement Programming
    • Implemented the two movement modes and the ability to swap between them
    • Used lerps to create a smooth zoom in of the camera
  • Audio
    • Created a grind sound effect to fade in/out according to the player's contact with the wave
    • Smoothly adjusted the music track when the wavelength changes (mostly)
    • Added a BGM and SFX volume slider to the main menu
