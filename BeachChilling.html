VR Meditation Experience

I worked with Liminal VR, a Melbourne based VR company as part of a university course. Working in a team of 4 students, we designed and developed a beach-based meditation experience called 'Beach Chilling'. Lacking artists, our group struggled finding free assets that worked well in VR but nonetheless, we were proud of what we ended up creating. It was a great learning experience, working professionally on a project from start to finish.

My Contributions:

  • Client Communication
    • Communicated with Liminal VR nearly every week, sending through builds and communicating any known issues
  • QA
    • As part of weekly build submission, I would load and troubleshoot each build on a VR headset to identify any issues, e.g. framerate or mistimed audio
    • Fixed up simple problems and communicated any major problems to client and team
  • Audio
    • Implemented a script to manage the audio sources throughout the experience
    • Wrote and recorded the meditation script and voiceover
    • Edited bird, tree, wave and wind sounds using Audacity
  • Sun Movement
    • Implemented a simple script for moving the sun according to the experience length
