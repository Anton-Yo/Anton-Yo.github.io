Banana History

Bananas go further back in history than you might think. First records indicate that they existed at least 10,000 years ago! Find out more about how bananas became the household fruit they are today by journeying through the History of the Banana.

Banana Lifecycle

Many people have many misconceptions about the banana tree and its growth cycle. For example, did you know that bananas are a vegetable? Ok, that one is not entirely true, they are actually a fruit. However, there was a lie, bananas don’t grow on trees, but on a plant made from just leaves! This one is true! To find out more read up on the banana plant growth cycle!

Types of Bananas

There are many types of bananas out there, ranging from the classics found at every supermarket to some bizarre and obscure banana types. There’s a banana nicknamed the apple banana which, hypothetically tastes a little like an apple. Just wait until the bananas start changing colours…
