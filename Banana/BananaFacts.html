Cool Banana Facts

Fact #1

Bananas were supposedly named by Arabian slave traders who encountered old smaller variants of bananas. They called them “banan”, the Arabic word for finger, due to bananas at the time being almost that small.

Fact #2

According to the US Environmental Protection Agency, bananas are very high in potassium, a small fraction of which will always be radioactive. This means that bananas emit .01 millirem of radiation which is not much. It would take eating 100 bananas to equal the amount of natural environmental radiation that the average person experiences daily.

Fact #3

Bananas are 75% water, no wonder they have to grow in such hot and humid conditions! As a bonus fact, bananas also can float in water! This means they are less dense then water, although it's a little more complicated than that. You can divide the banana up so some parts of the banana sink. Check out this!

Fact #4

Bananas are curved because when they are on the branch, they grow towards the sun! This is called negative geotropism which basically translates to “negative gravity”.
